Step 1: Register
Step 2: Add your profile details and social media accounts to increase your visibility
Step 3: On the mainpage, you can view upcoming voyages.
You can use the filter to specify trip dates, Vacancy and vehicle type.
Step 4: If you want to create Voyages, you need to register your vehicle(s).
Step 5: After registering your vehicle, you can create and announce your Voyages.

You need to specify the following:
   ⇒ Vacancy: how many people are you willing to host, (1)
   ⇒ Latitude and Longitude: The starting location for your voyage. Can be selected by clicking on the map, (2)
   ⇒ Trip start and end dates,(3)
   ⇒ Trip details: Duration, minimum contribution, and additional information (4)
   ⇒ Auction end date: Until when people can place their bids. (5)
   ⇒ Auction image: An image that describes your voyage, can be the same as your vehicle image (6)
Step 6: Create your itinerary by adding waypoints.
Step 7: Others will see your voyage like this.
   ⇒ They can click on your profile (1)
   ⇒ Or your vehicle (2)
Step 8: You can place your bid with price and a private message to the host.
Step 9: Only the host will be able to view your private message
Step 10: You can message other users on their profile pages and follow up on the messages tab in the navigation bar.
